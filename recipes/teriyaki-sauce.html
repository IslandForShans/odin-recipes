Simple Teriyaki Sauce

Description

A simple Japanese traditional sauce. This sauce is Umami, which means it is both sweet and savory. Perfect for soups and grilled meats!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 5 teaspoons packed brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon honey, or more to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon galric powder
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup cold water

Steps

  1. Combine 1 cup of water, soy sauce, brown sugar, honey, ginger, and garlic powder in a saucepan over medium heat. Cook until evenly heated through, about 1 minute.
  2. Mix cornstarch and 1/4 cup cold water together in a cup; stir until dissolved. Add to the saucepan. Cook and stir sauce until thickened, 5 to 7 minutes.
